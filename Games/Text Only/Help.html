RR - Rock

GG - Grass

↳ Walkable tile

SS - Sand

↳ Walkable tile often around water

WW - Water

↳ Non-walkable tile

== - Bridge

↳ Walkable tile for crossing water

*- - Lever

↳ Solid tile for opening and closing doors

|| - Door

↳ Solid tile that can be opened with levers (bridge when opened)

=> - Laser

↳ Solid tile that emits a laser to the right

== - Laser beam

↳ Beam that can activate levers

// - Mirror

↳ Redirects laser beams

88 - Teleport

↳ Tile which teleports you to the corresponding teleport tile
